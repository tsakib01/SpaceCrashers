Our Goal

SpaceCrashers is here to provide our customers with the space they need to get their work done and also help those customers who want to put their spare place into some use. The ultimate goal is to help both ends and put a spare place into some action.

Our Story

It is important for most people to find a place in their neighbourhoood or in the city or somewhere close to them to perform a certain task. After taking account of different people's struggle to find a place to perform their desired activities, SpaceCrashers started it's journey in 2022 to reduce the effort of finding the right place for the right task.
